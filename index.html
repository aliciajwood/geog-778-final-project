United States Incarceration Rates Explorer

  • About

    • Welcome to the United States Incarceration Rates Explorer! This application enables users to explore incarceration data and educate themselves on disparities within incarceration across the US.

      Expand the 'Toggle Variables' section below to change the layer displayed in the map view. Hover over the (i) icons to read variable definitions.

      Once a layer is displayed, click on features in the map to open up popups with charts displaying the variable across time, gender, and race/ethnicity.

      Use the buttons on the far right of the map view to search and zoom to a particular location, open the legend, and export a snapshot of the current map view.

      Created by Alicia Wood for GEOG 778, UW-Madison, 2024
